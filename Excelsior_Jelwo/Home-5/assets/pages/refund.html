Refund Policy

Returns

30-Day Return Window
We have a 30-day return policy, which means you have 30 days after receiving your item to request a return.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it — unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You'll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

To start a return, you can contact us at support@store.com. If your return is accepted, we'll send you a return shipping label, as well as instructions on how and where to send your package.

  • Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted.
  • Return shipping costs are borne by the customer unless the item is defective.
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

Damages and Issues

Please inspect your order upon reception and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged, or if you receive the wrong item, so that we can evaluate the issue and make it right.

We will work with you to resolve any issue as quickly as possible. To this end, we have developed our internal quality-review program to ensure all items leave our facility in perfect condition.

Exchanges

The fastest way to ensure you get what you want is to return the item you have, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item.

  • Examples of exchangeable items: Unworn jewelry in original packaging with all tags attached and proof of purchase present.
  • Purpose of exchange: To provide you with the correct item or a preferred alternative within our catalogue.

We will notify you once we've received and inspected your return, and let you know if the exchange was approved or not.

If approved, you'll be automatically refunded on your original payment method within 10 business days. To this end, we have developed Inditex's The List program.

Contact Us

You can always contact us for any return question at support@store.com.

Our support team is available Monday – Saturday, 10 AM to 6 PM IST. We aim to respond to all queries within one business day.

  • Provide your order number in all communications for faster resolution.
  • Attach clear photos of any damage or defect when reporting an issue.

How to Return

  1. 01 Email support@store.com with your order number and reason for return within 30 days of delivery.
  2. 02 Our team reviews your request and sends a prepaid return label (for defective items) or instructions for customer-paid returns.
  3. 03 Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all tags intact and drop it off at the designated courier point.
  4. 04 Once we receive and inspect the return, we notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund within 3 business days.
  5. 05 Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 10 business days.

Exceptions / Non-Returnable Items

Certain types of items cannot be returned. Please get in touch if you have questions or concerns about your specific item.

Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns on sale items or gift cards. To this end, we have developed Inditex's The List program.

Refunds

  • Examples of personal information collected: Order ID, payment method details, and the reason for the refund request.
  • Purpose of collection: To process your refund accurately and communicate the outcome to you.

We will notify you once we've received and inspected your return. If approved, you'll be automatically refunded on your original payment method.

Please remember it can take some time for your bank or credit card company to process and post the refund. If more than 15 business days have passed since we approved your return, please contact us at support@store.com.

To this end, we have developed Inditex's The List program to ensure your refund experience is smooth and transparent.
